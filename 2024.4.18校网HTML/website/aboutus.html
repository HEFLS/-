Hangzhou Entel Foreign Language School

Founded in 1993, Hangzhou Entel Foreign Language School is a private, co-educational, day school located in Hangzhou, China. The school offers a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in university and beyond.

Academics

The school's academic program is designed to challenge and inspire students. The curriculum includes a wide range of subjects, including English, math, science, social studies, Chinese, and foreign languages. Students are also encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities, such as sports, music, and art.

College Preparatory Program

Hangzhou Entel Foreign Language School offers a college preparatory program that helps students prepare for success in university. The program includes a rigorous academic curriculum, as well as a variety of support services, such as college counseling and tutoring.

Student Life

Hangzhou Entel Foreign Language School offers a rich student life experience. The school has a variety of clubs and organizations, as well as a strong athletic program. Students are also encouraged to participate in community service and other extracurricular activities.
